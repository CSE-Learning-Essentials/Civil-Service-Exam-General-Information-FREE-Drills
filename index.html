Section 4: General Information - Part 2: R.A. 6713

Passing Score: 8/10

Direction: Choose the correct answer from the choices given.

1. What is the primary objective of Republic Act No. 6713?

  • A) To develop comprehensive civil service training programs
  • B) To standardize salaries and benefits of government employees
  • C) To establish ethical guidelines and accountability measures in public service
  • D) To reorganize government agencies for improved efficiency

2. Under Republic Act No. 6713, what must public officials do upon assuming office?

  • A) Register to vote
  • B) Submit a performance report
  • C) Take a vacation leave
  • D) File a Statement of Assets, Liabilities, and Net Worth (SALN)

3. Which of the following is not a value promoted under Republic Act No. 6713?

  • A) Professionalism in public dealings and service delivery
  • B) Commitment to the public interest above personal gain
  • C) Responsiveness to the needs and concerns of the public
  • D) Strategic loyalty to political leadership for policy alignment

4. How often should public officials and employees update their SALN under Republic Act No. 6713?

  • A) Annually
  • B) Only upon retirement
  • C) Every five years
  • D) When promoted

5. Which office is responsible for monitoring compliance with Republic Act No. 6713?

  • A) Department of Justice
  • B) Civil Service Commission
  • C) Commission on Audit
  • D) Bureau of Internal Revenue

6. What does "Justness and Sincerity" require from public officials under Republic Act No. 6713?

  • A) To be friendly with any political affiliation
  • B) To treat everyone fairly and perform duties faithfully
  • C) To increase revenue for their office in order to extend the services
  • D) To always agree with their superiors and government officials

7. What penalty can be imposed for violations of Republic Act No. 6713?

  • A) Issuance of a formal warning with no further action
  • B) Compulsory resignation with loss of benefits
  • C) Suspension, dismissal, or other administrative sanctions
  • D) Public apology and voluntary ethics seminar attendance

8. Which of the following is encouraged under the value of "Nationalism and Patriotism" in Republic Act No. 6713?

  • A) Supporting international programs over national initiatives
  • B) Leveraging public office for recognition and popularity
  • C) Limiting involvement in local civic and cultural activities
  • D) Demonstrating loyalty to the Constitution and upholding national ideals

9. What does Republic Act No. 6713 say about receiving gifts?

  • A) Accepting tokens of appreciation is permitted without limitation
  • B) Receiving gifts is acceptable if it enhances public relations
  • C) Soliciting or accepting gifts related to official duties is strictly prohibited
  • D) Gifts may be accepted during recognized cultural or holiday celebrations only

10. What should public officials avoid to maintain "Political Neutrality" under Republic Act No. 6713?

  • A) Participating in non-partisan government conferences
  • B) Publicly supporting or endorsing candidates while in public office
  • C) Releasing official information through authorized channels
  • D) Submitting financial documents to relevant government agencies
